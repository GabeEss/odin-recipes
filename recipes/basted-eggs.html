Basted Eggs

Description

The basted egg is a simple fix for your breakfast or snacking needs.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch flaky sea salt

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Carefully crack eggs into the skillet and let cook until whites have almost set, about 1 minute.
  3. Add water, cover skillet with a lid or cookie sheet, reduce heat to medium-low, and let cook until whites have set, yolks are still runny, and some of hite has set over yolk, 1 to 2 more minutes.
  4. Remove lid and transfer eggs to a serving place. Garnish with pepper and flaky salt, if desired.
